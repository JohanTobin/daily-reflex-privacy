Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-03

Daily Reflex (“the app”) is a simple reaction time game. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled.

a) Personal Data

Daily Reflex does not collect or store any personal information such as name, email address, or account details.

b) Usage Data

The app processes reaction time data locally on your device and does not transmit or store it externally.

c) Advertising Data

Daily Reflex uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.

Google AdMob may collect device information, advertising identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

More information can be found at:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Information collected by Google AdMob is used to display and improve advertising. The developer of Daily Reflex does not directly access or store this data.
